Sponsorship Policy

A reference guide for evaluating inbound partnership and sponsorship opportunities across all Dina & Agnes platforms.

Core test — apply to every opportunity
Would our audience see this brand in a video and think "yes, obviously they use that"? If there is any hesitation, the answer is no.
✓ Accept immediately — pursue actively Zero brand friction. Natural to the channel. Apply proactively, do not wait to be approached.

Rate guidance (June 2026)

At 1.15k subscribers, negotiate based on niche value not reach. Mid-roll integration in a relevant tutorial: £200–250. Dedicated long-form sponsored video (only if format risk is justified): £400–500. Always define usage rights clearly. Organic posting and paid amplification are separate rights requiring separate fees.

Usage rights — plain English

The initial rate covers the right to post the video organically on our channel. If the brand wants to run it as a paid advertisement or boost it in campaigns, that requires a separate negotiation. Our rate for paid usage rights should reflect the video's performance after organic publication.

Format rule

Never accept a dedicated long-form video for a brand that only justifies a mid-roll mention. A 10–15 minute video built around a sponsor is a format risk on a growing channel. Protect editorial integrity. A mid-roll in a relevant tutorial is always the preferred format unless the content genuinely warrants a full video.
